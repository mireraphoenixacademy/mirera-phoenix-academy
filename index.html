About Us

Mirera Phoenix Academy is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a positive learning environment for all students. Our mission is to inspire lifelong learners and encourage academic excellence.

Founded in 2023, we emphasize the importance of holistic education, blending academics with extracurricular activities.

Academic Programs

  • Primary Education: Comprehensive curriculum focusing on foundational skills.
  • Secondary Education: Diverse subjects with opportunities for specialization.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Sports, arts, and clubs to enhance personal development.
